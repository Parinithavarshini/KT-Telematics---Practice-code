About us

KT Telematics is a technology company that focuses on vehicle tracking and fleet management solutions. It was founded in 2016 and is headquartered in Salem, Tamil Nadu, India. The company provides advanced GPS tracking, telematics, and IoT-based solutions that help businesses monitor and manage their vehicles in real time.KT Telematics develops systems that allow companies to track vehicle location, monitor fuel consumption, analyze driver behavior, and improve route planning. These technologies help transportation and logistics companies increase efficiency, enhance safety, and reduce operational costs.
